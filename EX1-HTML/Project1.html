OUR HISTORY





  World of Watches is a friends owned watch retail store with one of the biggest portfolio of brands to offer. The two similar minded fashion freeks or trendsetters started this store in 2018. The young entrepreneurs took up this profession as part time work. With its inception in 2018, WOW was one of the latest, famous and successful players to enter the watch retail business. The company was founded by a watchlover on the belief of providing quality timepieces with an equally important focus on after sales of these products. With this belief our proprietors have taken the company forward and expanded it within a short span of 3 years.
